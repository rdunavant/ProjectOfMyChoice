SHELBY FOREST

This is an animal that lives in Shelby Forest. If you visit Meeman-Shelby State Park, or Shelby Forest, you may see this animal. They are shy so if and when you do see one, they may be running across a trail or road in front of you.

Other animals that can be found in the park include, but are not limited to, warblers, grosbeaks, goldfinches, anhingas, yellow-crowned night herons, egrets, owls, woodpeckers, wild turkeys, king snakes, milk snakes, ringneck snakes, racers, worm snakes, ribbon snakes, green snakes, water snakes, hognose snakes, deer, coyotes, and red foxes
